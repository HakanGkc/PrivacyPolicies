Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01
Applies to the mobile application Çilek Akademi.

This Privacy Policy explains how Teymek Elektronik Yazılım Mühendislik (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you or your child use the mobile application Çilek Akademi - Eğitici Oyunlar (the “App”).

1. Audience and children’s privacy

The App is designed for children and families. We recognise the importance of protecting the privacy and safety of children online. We comply with applicable children’s privacy regulations, including the Google Play Families Policy and, where applicable, COPPA.

Parents and guardians should read this Privacy Policy carefully and ensure that they understand what data may be collected and how it is used.

2. Information we collect

2.1. Information you provide directly

The App does not require children to create an account or provide personal information such as name, address, or phone number.

If you contact us by email (for example, for support or feedback), we will receive the email address and any information you include in the message. This information is used only to respond to your request.

2.2. Information collected automatically

When you or your child use the App, certain information may be collected automatically by the platform and third-party services integrated into the App. This may include:

  • Device type, operating system version, and language settings
  • Anonymous identifiers (for example, an advertising identifier or instance ID)
  • App usage data such as screens visited, session duration, crashes, and performance data
  • Approximate location (country or region) inferred from IP address

This information is collected solely to operate and improve the App, to show appropriate content and ads, and to ensure security and stability.

3. Advertising and analytics

The App may display ads that are compliant with the Google Play Families Policy and are suitable for children and families. We do not allow interest-based or behavioural ads targeted to children based on their profile or activity over time.

Third-party advertising and analytics partners may collect and use limited data (such as device identifiers and usage data) to:

  • Deliver contextual ads (for example, based on the content of the App)
  • Measure the performance and effectiveness of ads
  • Understand how the App is used and improve its quality and stability

We instruct our partners to use such data only in compliance with children’s privacy requirements and to avoid creating profiles for behavioural advertising targeting children.

4. In-app purchases

The App may offer in-app purchases (for example, removing ads or unlocking additional content). Payments are processed by the app store (Google Play) and we do not receive or store credit card numbers or other sensitive payment information.

Parents and guardians can control or restrict in-app purchases through their device or Google Play account settings.

5. How we use information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App
  • To display child-appropriate and family-friendly ads
  • To fix bugs, prevent fraud, and ensure security and integrity
  • To comply with legal obligations and app store policies
  • To respond to support requests and communicate with parents or guardians

6. Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers and partners who help us provide the App, such as analytics, crash reporting, and advertising services.
  • Authorities or regulators if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, users’ safety, or comply with legal obligations.

All such partners are required to handle data in accordance with applicable privacy laws and children’s privacy requirements.

7. Data retention

We keep data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. When data is no longer needed, we will delete it or anonymise it in a secure manner.

8. Children’s rights and parental controls

Parents or legal guardians have the right to:

  • Request information about the data collected through the App
  • Request deletion of data that may be linked to their child
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided in the “Contact us” section below. We may need to verify your identity as a parent or guardian.

9. International transfers

Our servers and those of our service providers may be located in different countries. As a result, the information collected through the App may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We take appropriate measures to protect this data in accordance with applicable law.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information processed in connection with the App against loss, misuse, and unauthorised access. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Third-party links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage parents and guardians to review the privacy policies of any third-party services used by their children.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect information.
